ON FILM

QHacks 2018

As the Graphic Designer and Videographer for QHacks 2019, I had to create a video encapsulating the 36-hour hackathon weekend held at Queen's University. Participants came from Universities all across North America, and took part in games, workshops and lots of hacking.

Queen's University Computing Orientation Executives 2018

The 2018 Queen's Computing Orientation Executives were introduced to the incoming students with an introduction video played at Welcome Forum during Orientation Week 2018.

Queen's University Focus Film Festival 2018

During Kingston's Focus Film Festival 2018, participants had 72 hours to write, shoot and edit a 5 minute short centered around the theme "Focus Jukebox". The song given to this group was "The One That Got Away" by Katy Perry. In the end, we got 5 nominations including Best Cinematography, Editing, Sound, Production Design, and Picture - winning both Best Sound & Best Picture.
