About

21 year old Toronto-based photographer, videographer and computer science student at Queen's University. My hobbies are painting and photography, and my passion for graphic design grew after I became the Head of Publications for Queen's University Orientation Week 2018. From that experience, I continued my interest through the role of Graphic Designer and Media Coordinator for QHacks 2019. In addition to my studies, I completed a 10-month internship as a UI/UX Designer at North60 Technologies in Toronto.
